Before You Travel to China
Everything You Need to Know
Practical tips for first-time visitors to Shanghai.
📱
Download a VPN
Google, Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp are blocked in China. Install a VPN before you go — ExpressVPN and NordVPN work well.
Essential
💳
Set Up Alipay
China is cashless — payments are by QR code only. Set up WeChat Pay or Alipay before you go. Few places accept international cards.
Important
🛂
Visa
Mongolian passport holders can enter visa-free for up to 30 days. Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 more months.
Know This
📡
SIM Card or Pocket WiFi
Get a Chinese SIM card or pocket WiFi. Available at the airport. Mongolian SIM roaming is expensive.
Tips
🏥
Travel Insurance
Emergency medical costs in China are very high. Always get travel insurance. Notify us in advance if you plan a VIP health check.
Safety
🗺️
Maps
Google Maps doesn't work in China. Use Baidu Maps or Apple Maps. Gaode Maps (高德地图) has the most accurate maps.
Navigation
🚇
Metro
Shanghai's metro is cheap, fast, and reliable. Get a Jiaotong card — valid at all stations and buses. Card deposit is 20 CNY.
Transport
🌐
Language
English is rarely spoken in China. Google Translate's camera feature is very useful. With a Mongolian guide, there's no language barrier.
Communication
🏨
Hotel
Passport details are required when booking. Choose hotels that accept foreign guests — Airbnb is restricted in China.
