✈️ Airport Connections

Shanghai has two airports. Here's how to get to the city from each one.

🛬
Pudong (PVG)

International Airport

Metro Line 2: ~60 min to city center, 7–9¥
Maglev Train: 8 minutes to Longyang Rd, 50¥
Taxi: ~200¥ to Puxi (45–90 min depending on traffic)

🛫
Hongqiao (SHA)

Domestic + Some International

Metro Line 2 or 10: Direct to city, ~30–40 min, 4–6¥
Taxi: ~100¥ to Puxi
Also: Hongqiao Railway Station is next door — convenient for high-speed rail

💡 Tip: If you have a lot of luggage, take a taxi from the airport. Our team can arrange VIP airport pickup — no stress, Mongolian-speaking driver. Book airport pickup →
🎫 Metro Card

The easiest way to ride — buy a Shanghai Public Transportation Card (交通卡).

💳

Where to Get It

Buy at any metro station service desk or convenience store (Family Mart, 7-Eleven) inside the station.

💰

Cost

20¥ deposit (refundable when you return the card)
Minimum top-up: 20¥
Works on metro, bus, and some ferries.

📱

Alternatively

Use WeChat Pay or Alipay QR code directly at the metro turnstile — no card needed if you have a working digital wallet.

📱 Paying with WeChat / Alipay

You can pay metro fares directly with your phone — no card needed.

1

Open WeChat or Alipay

Make sure you have a working Chinese bank card or international card linked to your account.

2

Find "Metro QR Code" / "地铁码"

In WeChat: Wallet → Transport Card. In Alipay: Metro / 地铁.

3

Scan at Turnstile

Hold your QR code up to the scanner at the gate. Works on entry and exit.

4

Fare Deducted Automatically

Exact fare is charged based on distance when you exit.

💡 International travelers: Alipay now accepts international Visa/Mastercard — set it up before your trip! WeChat Pay may require a Chinese bank card for some features.

🌙 Night Transport

Metro runs until ~23:00. For late nights, here are your options.

🚖

DiDi (滴滴)

China's Uber. English app available. Download before arriving — most convenient late-night option.

🚕

Street Taxi

Flag a yellow taxi on the street. Starting fare ~14¥. Show your destination in Chinese characters.

🚌

Night Bus

Night routes (夜宵线) run on some lines after metro closes. Cheap but infrequent.

🚲

Hellobike

Dockless bike-share. Great for short trips under 3km. Scan QR with Alipay.

💡 LFS Tip: Save DiDi on your phone before arriving in Shanghai. It's the safest and easiest night transport — English interface and GPS tracking.
